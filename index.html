Who

San Francisco is a neo-grotesque sans-serif typeface made by Apple Inc.

When

It was first released to developers on November 18, 2014.

Where

It is system typeface of the Apple Watch, OS X and iOS with OS X El Capitan and iOS 9.

Who

Roboto is a neo-grotesque sans-serif typeface family developed by Google. Designer is Christian Robertson.

When

It was officially made available for free download on January 12, 2012, on the newly launched Android Design website.

Where

It is system typeface on the Android operating system, and since 2013 it is also the default font of other Google services such as Google+, Google Play, YouTube and Google Maps.
